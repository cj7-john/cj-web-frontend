Our Story

Built from Durban, for the World

CJ Web Studio started with a simple belief: South African businesses deserve world-class websites that actually drive results — not template filler with generic stock photos and page builder bloat.

Every line of code is handwritten. Every design is purpose-built around the client's brand and audience. We don't use builders because your business isn't generic — and your website shouldn't be either.

From 3D interactive experiences to high-converting multi-page business sites, we've helped 50+ businesses establish a powerful digital presence that grows with them.
